Identity and Values

learning plan:

  • my long term goal is to work as a full stack developer, and as alot of the people here to have some independence when it comes to how and where I work. I have considered further down the line going to study and learn some other fields, such as cyber security. However my medium term goal is to work as a full stack developer for a few years atleast.

However to contrast I find once I get started on something that I find it hard to stop, I want to keep working on it, and I probably would until I deem it finished, however that seems like an endless endevour sometimes as I am of the opinion that my work can always be improved.

I wish to have freedom where and how I work, and that relies heavily on communication with a team or project manager, as face to face and constantly being im the same office all the time wouldn't be as accesible of an option. That being said I don't have much knowledge about that environment and I could be very wrong. All in all I just want to be able to work well in a team, and to manage my time well. Those would be the biggest things for me coming through and out of Dev Academy.
  • I will manage myeslf to work productively by executing the time management tools that have been presented so far through foundations. I will make sure to treat people with respect and to communicate clearly on any topic that is presented to a team.

When that doesn't work for me, I will then go to my cohort and peers and ask there. If noone has the answer I will then go to the facilitators and mentors and ask there. That is my commitment on how I will seek help in a timely way.

Me personally I need facilitators to help me when I get stuck, and this is best done by pointing me in the right direction rather than giving a direct solution, as I know ill forget it if its told to my face. I have noticed that that is how the dev academy facilitators already help in the tech support channel so that works well for me.
  • I have little scheduling commitments as I am young and dont have many family commitments, so that won't become a problem for me.
